Portfolio Case — Identity Rebuild for a Creator

Before: scattered advice, broad audience, low conversion. After: niche identity, aligned offers, higher quality leads.

Process

  1. Audit existing content and identify recurring themes.
  2. Choose the identity anchor (example: "Content systems for B2B founders").
  3. Reformat posts to show process, not tips. Add a direct CTA for discovery calls.

Results

Three months later the creator had a pipeline of three paid projects and a clear case study to show. The conversion rate on outreach messages rose from 2 percent to 12 percent.

Takeaway

Clarity increases conversion. Specificity filters the wrong viewers and draws the right ones closer.
