Signals — The Notebook of Micro Insights

Micro notes are the food for experiments. Keep five short signals every day: one observation, one question, one failure, one small win, one idea to try.

How to use this notebook

  • Collect: Save short items when they occur.
  • Cluster: Weekly, group similar notes into a theme.
  • Publish: Turn the cluster into one experiment or one essay.

Signals are small. Systems turn them into output. Keep both honest.
