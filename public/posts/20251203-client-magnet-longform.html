Client Magnet — How I Turn Experiments into Paying Clients

Clients do not buy content. They buy predictable outcomes. A client magnet is a predictable set of signals, proof, and a simple path to hire you. Here is the system.

Signal

Your signal is the identity you publish. It must be narrow and repeated.

Proof

Proof is public experiments, case studies, and consistent logging of results. One strong case study is worth ten vague testimonials.

Path

Make the path to hire obvious: a single CTA that leads to a discovery conversation. Reduce friction. The CTA should ask them for one clear piece of information that qualifies them.

System

  1. Publish 3 experiments per month
  2. Package one experiment per quarter into a case study
  3. Use one CTA only — "Start your experiment" — and link it to an email or simple form

CTA: Want a free short audit for fit? Email hi@youremail.com with "Audit: Client Magnet".
